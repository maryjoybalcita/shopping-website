K-Beauty (Korean: 케이뷰티; RR: Keibyuti) is an umbrella term for skincare products that are derived from South Korea.
The fad gained popularity worldwide, especially in East Asia, Southeast Asia, South Asia, and the Western world,
and focuses on health, hydration, and an emphasis for brightening effects.
